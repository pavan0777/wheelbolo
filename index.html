    A random picker wheel that just works

    Wheel Bolo is a free spin the wheel tool — a colourful random name picker and decision wheel you can use for anything that needs a fair, unbiased choice. Type a list of names or options, give the wheel a spin, and watch it glide to a stop on one lucky winner with a burst of confetti. There is nothing to install and nothing to sign up for.

    How to use the wheel

    1. Add your entries. Type one name per line in the box, or paste a comma-separated list. The wheel updates instantly as you type.
    2. Choose a mode. Use Random pick when the winner can be chosen again, or Elimination to remove each winner after they are picked — perfect for raffles and tournaments.
    3. Spin. Tap the centre of the wheel or the big Spin button. The winner is highlighted and announced.
    4. Share. Hit Share result to send a winner card to WhatsApp, or Copy link to share the exact wheel you built — your list travels in the link, no account needed.

    Why people love it

    Teachers use it to call on students fairly. Families use it to settle what to cook for dinner or who does the dishes. Office teams use it for lucky draws and standup picks. Sports fans use it to assign teams. Because everything happens in your browser and your list lives only in the link you share, it is fast, private, and works beautifully on a phone — which is exactly how most people use it.

    Fair and random by design

    Each spin picks a winner using your browser's cryptographic random number generator, so every entry has an equal chance every single time. No hidden weighting, no tracking of who won before. Reload the page and the wheel forgets everything.
